30-Day Fit Guarantee

Not the perfect fit? Exchange for free within 30 days. No questions asked.

We believe the right fit shouldn't be a gamble. Every KaySneakers pair is crafted with dual-density comfort and thoughtful sizing, but feet are unique. If your new shoes don't feel perfect, we'll exchange them—free of charge—within 30 days of delivery.

How it works

  1. Contact us at support@kaysneakers.com or use our chat
  2. Tell us your order number and desired size/width
  3. We'll ship your exchange with a prepaid return label
  4. Send back the original pair within 14 days of receiving your exchange

Conditions

  • Shoes must be unworn and in original packaging
  • One exchange per order
  • Available for US orders; international may vary
